About Me

I'm a software developer with a background in HR and Analytics. My move into tech wasn't accidental — working in HR exposed me to the friction of manual, repetitive processes, and I became convinced that code was the most powerful tool to fix them. I completed the Founders and Coders bootcamp, where I built full-stack applications using React, TypeScript, Node.js, and AI APIs.

Most recently, I competed in the HuddleHive Women in Tech Hackathon, where my team built QCare — an NHS waiting list management platform that gives patients real-time queue visibility and automates escalations to reduce Did Not Attend rates. I bring technical ability, business acumen, and a genuine drive to build things that work for real people.
